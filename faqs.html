FAQ's
What is the purpose of this study?

This study aims to understand how environmental conditions, such as air pollution, extreme heat, and access to greenspace, affect mental health and well-being.

Who is eligible to participate?

Adults who live in Erie and Niagara regions and who are willing to share information about their daily environment, mental health, and/or lived experiences may be eligible.

What will participation involve?

Depending on the study component, participants may be asked to complete surveys, share experiences related to environmental stressors, and/or carry a small portable sensor.

How will my data be protected?

Your privacy and data security are a top priority. All information collected is stored securely and is used only for research purposes.
